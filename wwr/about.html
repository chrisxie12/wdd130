Our History

Founded in 2012, our company began as a dream to share the thrill of the river. Over time, we grew from one raft and a bucket of paddles to a fleet of adventures that span multiple rivers and terrains.

Our experienced guides are passionate about safety and about connecting you with the unforgettable beauty of the water. We are proud to have guided thousands of rafters through exciting rapids and serene stretches of river alike.
