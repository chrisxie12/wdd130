About Us - Explore Africa

At Explore Africa, our mission is to bring people closer to nature, foster teamwork, and deliver unforgettable moments on the river through our white water rafting adventures. Whether you're a seasoned rafter or a first-timer.

History

Founded in 2012, our company began as a dream to share the thrill of the river. Over time, we grew from one raft and a bucket of paddles to a fleet of adventures that span multiple rivers and terrains. Our experienced guides are passionate about safety.
