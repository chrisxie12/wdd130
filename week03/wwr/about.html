Offin River - Kumasi Rafting & Adventure Tours

At Offin River Adventures, our mission is to bring thrill, nature, and culture together. We believe every rafting trip is a journey into the heart of Ghana’s beauty. Whether you are here for the adrenaline rush or the peaceful river views, our team ensures unforgettable experiences.

History

Founded in Kumasi, Offin River - Kumasi Rafting & Adventure Tours started with a dream to showcase the adventurous spirit of the Ashanti Region. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a trusted name for river expeditions and eco-tourism in Ghana.
