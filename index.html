Kumasi

Kumasi is a historic city in the Ashanti Region of Ghana. Known as the “Garden City,” it is famous for its vibrant culture, bustling markets, and rich traditions such as kente weaving and wood carving. The city is also home to the Manhyia Palace and serves as the seat of the Ashanti King.
