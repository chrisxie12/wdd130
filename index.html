Ghana

Ghana is a vibrant country in West Africa known for its rich history, diverse cultures, and welcoming people. From the bustling markets of Accra to the historic castles along the coast, Ghana offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity. Kumasi, the cultural capital, is home to the Ashanti Kingdom and famous for its crafts and festivals.
